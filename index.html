My Story

From Inspiration to Creation

I am Victoria, an aspiring 3D modelling student. I have lived in four different countries over the years, namely Indonesia, Japan, Hong Kong and now, Singapore. Growing up, films and games made up a major part of my childhood. The fluidity of art and freedom of creativity are my greatest motivations for me to pursue a career in the artistic industry.

In my free time I enjoy immersing myself into the world of RPG/MOBA games and drawing. My current goal is to land myself a foot in the industry by getting employed in a film or games studio.
